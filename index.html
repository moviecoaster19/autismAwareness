    Introducing Autism

    Have you ever met anyone who behaves or reacts differently? Such as not responding or saying hello back right away? That means they might have autism, which means their brains work differently compared to that of neurotypicals.

    Spreading the Word...

    While autism may sound scary or serious, it is nothing like that. Those with autism display different behaviorial patterns that may make them seem isolated, but this is really their unique way of experiencing the world around us.
